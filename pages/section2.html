Postcards from a Dream: Imagining the World Through Art and Poetry

Abstract

This piece is a collection of imagined travels told through postcards and poetry. Each postcard captures a different destination with haikus layered over images that echo the feeling of the place. While I haven’t physically visited these locations, I explored them through travel vlogs, stories, and photos and pieced together a journey made from both someone else’s footsteps and my own imagination.

The idea came from a memory: FaceTiming a friend studying abroad in Scotland, and I felt connected to a place I’d never been. That moment reminded me that we can experience the world in many ways. Sometimes it's a screen, and sometimes it's through stories.

Each haiku distills a place into a few quiet lines. By pairing them with curated imagery, the postcards are meant to feel like poetic snapshots or tiny souvenirs from travels that exist somewhere between real and reimagined.

Village of Oia: Santorini, Greece

Santorini Blues
White homes kiss the sky,
domes shine bright in endless blue,
sunlight paints the sea.

Golden sands below,
waves whisper against the shore,
warm breeze on my skin.

Lost in paradise,
sipping wine as sun sinks low,
relaxation in salty air.

The Eiffel Tower: Paris, France

Parisian Dreams
Soft breeze, quiet streets,
morning light on café cups,
whispers in the air.

Eiffel stands so tall,
watching lovers hand in hand,
city full of dreams.

Wine, flowers, and art,
golden lights reflect and glow,
Paris feels like love.

Barrio de Jalatlaco: Oaxaca, Mexico

Hidden Gem of Oaxaca
Murals tell their tales,
walls alive with strokes of art,
stories paint the streets.

Pink, gold, green, and blue,
homes like canvases of light,
colors fill the sky.

Soft steps on warm stone,
breeze carries songs through the streets,
art and peace reside.

Burj Khalifa: Dubai, United Arab Emirates

Heart of Dubai
Burj Khalifa stands,
taller than the golden sun,
watching dunes below.

City of Wonders
Skyscrapers like waves,
rising from the desert sands,
palms and lights collide.

Luxury
Heat drapes over me,
lights shine, gold glows all around,
Dubai feels like wealth.
